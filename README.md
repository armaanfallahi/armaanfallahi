# Hi, I'm Armaan 👋

I'm an **MD–PhD student** at the **University of Toronto** and the **Hospital for Sick Children**, working in the Josselyn & Frankland labs. I study how **early-life experience** shapes the developing **memory circuits** of the brain — how *engrams* form in the amygdala, and how early-life adversity rewires threat learning.

I'm a CIHR Vanier Scholar and a Junior Fellow of Massey College. Alongside the bench, I write and advocate on the physician's role in society and mentor trainees on the clinician-scientist path.

### 🔬 Research interests
Memory engrams · early-life adversity · amygdala circuits · computational & systems neuroscience
